Project

Capstone Project

This is a capstone project from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology (DTETI) at Gadjah Mada University. The project delves into Small and Medium Enterprises (UMKM) in Indonesia, with a specific focus on Batik. The primary focus of this project is on quality control in Batik stores. The project includes features such as defect detection and sales forecasting for Batik stores. I employed YOLOv8 for defect detection and Exponential Smoothing for sales forecasting

Prophet Forecasting

This project represents my one-month internship at PT Telkomsel. The objective of the project is to compare three forecasting methods: LSTM, ARIMA, and Prophet. These methods are employed to analyze network traffic data from Telkomsel, aiming to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies. The project involves utilizing forecasting models to generate accurate predictions for network traffic, anticipating future patterns and demands.
